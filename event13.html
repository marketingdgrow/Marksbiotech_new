CONFERENCE 2024

1st National Implantology Conference – Bangalore

21st – 23rd June 2024 | Hotel Sheraton Grand, Rajaji Nagar, Bengaluru

Organized by the Osseointegration Society of India, the 1st National Implantology Conference marked a major milestone in advancing implant dentistry across India. Hosted at the prestigious Sheraton Grand, the three-day scientific gathering brought together leading implantologists, oral surgeons, prosthodontists, academicians, and industry pioneers from across the country.

Advancing Implant Dentistry in India

Focused on clinical precision, technological integration, and long-term implant success, the conference created a powerful platform for scientific exchange and hands-on exposure to next-generation implant solutions.

The event highlighted the growing importance of digital workflows, biologically driven implant protocols, and precision-based surgical planning in modern implant practice.

Our Participation at Booth 24

Marks Biotech proudly participated in this landmark event at Booth 24, showcasing advanced implant innovations in collaboration with global leaders:

  • B&B Dental Implant Company – Italy
  • Navident – Canada

Our booth attracted strong engagement from clinicians eager to explore modern surgical workflows and navigation-driven implantology.

Showcasing the Future of Implant Dentistry

Dynamic Navigation Systems

Real-time, computer-guided implant placement systems that enhance surgical accuracy, improve predictability, and minimize clinical risk through intraoperative tracking and precision control.

Static Guided Solutions

Digitally planned and template-assisted implant protocols designed to ensure precision, efficiency, and optimized prosthetic outcomes.

Integrated Digital Workflows

From treatment planning to final prosthetic placement, our solutions emphasized seamless digital integration, enabling clinicians to elevate treatment standards and long-term clinical success.

Conference Highlights

  • Insightful keynote sessions by renowned implantologists
  • Scientific paper and poster presentations
  • Clinical discussions on complex implant cases
  • Workshops focusing on emerging surgical techniques
  • Networking opportunities with top professionals

The event reinforced the growing importance of digital precision and biologically driven implant protocols in modern dentistry.

A Milestone Event

Our collaboration with B&B Dental Implant Company (Italy) and Navident (Canada) reflects our continued commitment to bringing globally recognized implant systems and navigation technologies to the Indian dental community.

The overwhelming response at Booth 24 demonstrated a clear shift toward technology-driven implantology — where accuracy, safety, and long-term success define clinical excellence.

We thank the Osseointegration Society of India, the organizing committee, and all the dental professionals who visited us and engaged with our solutions.

We look forward to continuing our mission of advancing implant dentistry through innovation, education, and collaboration.
