The Story of Marks Biotech

Marks Biotech Inc., has grown as a dedicated provider of advanced dental products and solutions, with a focus on supporting dental professionals across India since 2018.

Headquartered in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, with Multiple Branch Offices and Warehouses across India. Marks Biotech Inc. specializes in dental consumables, oral care products, rehabilitation solutions, and digital technology-enabled systems spanning multiple dental disciplines — including Maxillofacial Surgery, Periodontics, Prosthodontics, Endodontics, Oral Radiology and works closely with Dental Technicians.

Marks Biotech Inc. operates in the broader context of India's expanding MedTech and dental technology sector, where digital innovations — including AI, digital diagnostics, and guided workflows — play an increasingly important role in improving clinical outcomes.
