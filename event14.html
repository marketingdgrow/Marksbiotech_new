OSICON 2025 – 2nd National Conference of the Osseointegration Society of India

Jaipur | 31st October – 2nd November 2025

The 2nd National Conference of the Osseointegration Society of India (OSICON 2025) concluded successfully in Jaipur, bringing together leading implantologists, clinicians, researchers, and industry innovators from across the country.

About OSICON 2025

Centered around the theme “Osseointegration: From Foundation to Future”, the conference served as a powerful platform for scientific exchange, clinical discussions, and the exploration of next-generation implant technologies.

The event gathered some of the most respected voices in implant dentistry, creating an environment that fostered learning, collaboration, and innovation across academic and clinical domains.

Our Presence at OSICON 2025

We were proud to participate in this prestigious national event at Booth 104 & 105, where we showcased our advanced implant solutions and cutting-edge technologies in collaboration with global leaders in implant innovation.

Our booth attracted significant engagement from dental professionals, academic experts, and young clinicians eager to explore:

  • Advanced implant systems
  • Precision-driven surgical solutions
  • Innovative biomaterials
  • Digital workflow integration
  • Evidence-based clinical applications

The response was overwhelming, reaffirming the growing demand for high-quality, reliable implant solutions in the Indian market.

Scientific Excellence & Industry Innovation

OSICON 2025 featured insightful keynote lectures, panel discussions, scientific paper presentations, and hands-on sessions led by respected experts in implant dentistry.

The conference highlighted emerging trends in:

  • Surface technology advancements
  • Regenerative protocols
  • Guided implant surgery
  • Prosthetic precision
  • Long-term clinical predictability

The scientific depth and collaborative environment made this edition of OSICON a benchmark event in the implant dentistry landscape.

Strengthening Strategic Partnerships

Our participation also strengthened strategic collaborations with leading implant innovators including:

  • Navident
  • B&B Dental Implant Company (Italy)

These partnerships reflect our continued commitment to delivering world-class implant solutions backed by research, precision, and long-term clinical reliability.

A Successful Milestone

OSICON 2025 was more than a conference — it was a milestone in advancing implant dentistry in India. The exchange of ideas, the enthusiasm of young professionals, and the spirit of innovation demonstrated that the future of osseointegration is stronger than ever.

We extend our sincere gratitude to the Osseointegration Society of India, the organizing committee, and all the dental professionals who visited us at Booth 104 & 105.

We look forward to continuing our journey of innovation, collaboration, and excellence in implant dentistry.
